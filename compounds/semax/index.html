Research overview

A synthetic 7-amino-acid peptide based on a section of the stress hormone ACTH, with a stabilising tail added to improve its reach within biological systems. Preclinical studies have measured increased BDNF expression — a protein associated with brain cell growth and survival — in neuronal tissue following administration. Neuroprotective effects have also been studied in animal models of experimentally induced brain ischaemia. Widely researched in Eastern European neuroscience institutions. For in vitro research use only. HPLC purity 99.0%.

Summary paraphrased from the peer-reviewed preclinical literature. Full citations will be available in the research library. For in vitro research use only.
