Research overview

A synthetic peptide that simultaneously activates three distinct metabolic receptors — GLP-1, GIP, and glucagon — each involved in regulating energy balance, insulin response, and fat metabolism. By engaging all three pathways at once, it offers researchers a tool for studying multi-receptor metabolic signalling in ways that single-agonist compounds cannot. Among the most actively investigated next-generation metabolic research peptides. For in vitro research use only. HPLC purity 98.8%.
