Research overview

A synthetic analogue of the natural signal the brain uses to trigger growth hormone release. The absence of a Drug Affinity Complex (DAC) means it produces a shorter, sharper pulse of GH release — more closely mimicking the natural physiological pattern than longer-acting variants. Animal studies have measured its effects on GH and IGF-1 concentrations. Frequently studied alongside Ipamorelin in GH secretion research. For in vitro research use only. HPLC purity 99.1%.

Summary paraphrased from the peer-reviewed preclinical literature. Full citations will be available in the research library. For in vitro research use only.
