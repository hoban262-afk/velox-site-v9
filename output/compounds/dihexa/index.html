Research overview

A synthetic peptide derived from an angiotensin IV analogue, engineered to resist enzymatic breakdown for greater stability in biological environments. Preclinical research investigates its interaction with the HGF/c-Met signalling axis — a pathway associated with the formation and strengthening of synaptic connections between brain cells. Rodent experiments have examined dendritic spine density (a structural measure of synaptic connectivity) and spatial memory task performance in animal models. Among the newer compounds in cognitive neuroscience peptide research. For in vitro research use only. HPLC purity 98.5%.

Summary paraphrased from the peer-reviewed preclinical literature. Full citations will be available in the research library. For in vitro research use only.
