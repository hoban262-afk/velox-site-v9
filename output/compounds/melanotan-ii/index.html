Research overview

A synthetic analogue of alpha-MSH that binds to multiple melanocortin receptors (MC1R, MC3R, MC4R, MC5R) simultaneously. These receptors are involved in pigmentation, energy regulation, and neurological signalling. Animal studies have examined its effects on melanin production pathways and on receptor-mediated signalling across several tissue systems. Its ability to interact with multiple receptor subtypes makes it a useful tool for multi-pathway receptor research. For in vitro research use only. HPLC purity 99.3%.

Summary paraphrased from the peer-reviewed preclinical literature. Full citations will be available in the research library. For in vitro research use only.
