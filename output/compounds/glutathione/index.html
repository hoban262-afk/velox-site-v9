Research overview

A naturally occurring tripeptide antioxidant present in virtually every mammalian cell. It plays a central role in protecting cells from oxidative damage by neutralising free radicals, and is essential for mitochondrial function and cellular redox balance. Research in aged animal models documents measurable glutathione decline, and studies examine whether restoring levels influences DNA repair activity and oxidative stress markers. A widely used research reagent in ageing, mitochondrial, and cellular protection studies. For in vitro research use only. HPLC purity 99.2%.

Summary paraphrased from the peer-reviewed preclinical literature. Full citations will be available in the research library. For in vitro research use only.
