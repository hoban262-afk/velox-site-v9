Healing & Repair

BPC-157

Body Protection Compound-157
99.4% HPLC · CAS 137525-51-0

A 15-amino-acid synthetic peptide that is among the most extensively studied repair-focused compounds in preclinical science. Rodent studies have investigated its role in stimulating new blood vessel formation (angiogenesis), accelerating wound tissue closure, and protecting the stomach and intestinal lining from experimentally induced damage.

Cognitive

Semax

ACTH(4-7) Pro-Gly-Pro Analogue
99% HPLC · CAS 80714-61-0

A synthetic 7-amino-acid peptide based on a section of the stress hormone ACTH, with a stabilising tail added to improve its reach within biological systems. Preclinical studies have measured increased BDNF expression — a protein associated with brain cell growth and survival — in neuronal tissue following administration.

Metabolic

NAD+

Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide
99.5% HPLC · CAS 53-84-9

A coenzyme present in every living cell and essential for mitochondrial energy production and sirtuin enzyme activity. Sirtuins are proteins involved in DNA repair, gene regulation, and cellular stress responses.

£54.99 View compound →
Metabolic

Retatrutide

GLP-1 / GIP / Glucagon Triple Agonist
98.8% HPLC

A synthetic peptide that simultaneously activates three distinct metabolic receptors — GLP-1, GIP, and glucagon — each involved in regulating energy balance, insulin response, and fat metabolism. By engaging all three pathways at once, it offers researchers a tool for studying multi-receptor metabolic signalling in ways that single-agonist compounds cannot.

Anti-Ageing

GHK-Cu

Copper Tripeptide-1
99% HPLC · CAS 49557-75-7

A naturally occurring tripeptide found in mammalian blood that carries a copper atom. In skin cell and animal research models, it has been studied for its ability to upregulate the genes responsible for producing collagen and elastin — the structural proteins that give tissue its strength and elasticity.
